Research & Publications

Selected research interests, projects and publications of Dr. Ugochukw u Albert Elenwa, Ph.D. — organized for easy reading and citability.

Major Areas of Research Interest

  • School mapping, plant planning and management.
  • Educational planning process and techniques.
  • Educational policy analysis and quality service delivery.
  • Planning and management of change and innovation in the school system.
  • Politics in education, leadership and institutional governance.
  • Educational planning, the economy and national development.
  • Planning education for empowerment and sustainable development.
  • Institutional human resources professional development and school effectiveness.
